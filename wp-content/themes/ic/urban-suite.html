Brooklyn is a bustling borough with its own flavor. There are so many great cultural institutions here, such as Brooklyn Museum of Art, Brooklyn Academy of Music, Brooklyn Transit Museum and the Brooklyn Children’s Museum. Get a taste of what Brooklyn has to offer with our Culture Grows in Brooklyn package.

Location right on the beach was perfect. It was nice to pick up a free cup of coffee in the reception and take a stroll on the beach to watch seals and birds before breakfast.

Book a two night stay in our NU Standard Queen room featuring original art work by local Brooklyn artists and receive complimentary passes to enjoy the Brooklyn Museum and the MTA Transit Museum. With this package, you’ll also have use of NU’s Brooklyn Cruisers for your entire stay with which to explore our neighborhood parks, shops and restaurants. End each day of cultural pursuits with a relaxing complimentary Anti-Manhattan signature cocktail in Misdemeanor. This package is not available for Sunday or Monday stays as the museums are closed.
